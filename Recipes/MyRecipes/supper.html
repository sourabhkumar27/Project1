CURRY PASTA RECIPE

Pasta cooked with tomatoes, onions, cream and milk with the flavors of meat masala to make a curry pasta.

Ingredients
Quantity Ingredient Instruction
1 tbsp Oil
2 tbsn Garlic Crushed
1/2 cup Onions Chopped
2 Cups Tomato Puree
to taste Salt
2 tsp Sugar
2 tbsp Cream
1/2 cup Milk

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. In a pan add oil and crushed garlic.
  2. Add chopped onions. Saute till light golden.
  3. Add the tomato puree, meat masala, salt and sugar. Cook till the puree starts to leave sides.
  4. Add cream and milk. Mix till smooth. Add boiled pasta.
  5. Serve hot.
